About

Bhanso is a local business strongly believing in producing own products to sustain the community. Bhanso is an homemade kitchen accessories crafts started by Rita Tamrakar in 2018.

"To support the country's economy & in order to repair the leakage of the economy from Nepal to the other neighbouring nations. We need to produce and consume our own products which will help in country's economic growth and eventually help it grow as a nation".
